Port Moresby National High School

Port Moresby National High School (POMNATHS) was officially opened on the 1st of June, 1995 as a School of Excellence in Papua New Guinea (PNG). Our school is located in the capital city of PNG, Port Moresby. POMNATHS is one of the six (6) National High SChools in the country and the only day school. In addition, it is a co-education institution. Since its openening in the mid-90's POMNATHS has been one of the top schools in the country. The school has greatly contributed to the development of PNG by providing quality education and by supplying the country with much needed professional labour for the workforce.

The school is nestled in a large campus and has a conference centre within its premises. Apart from the compulsory high school subjects, the school aslo tries to teach Christian values to the students through Religious Education classes conducted as part of the extracurricular activities every Wednesday afternoons. The school also encourages physical enrichment by conducting sporting activities on Thursdays. Furthermore, POMNATHS being a melting pot of diverse cultures proudly hosts its annual cultural show known as the Hahenamo where the students from the 22 provinces showcase their culture through traditional singing and dancing.

The Government of Japan as a gift built Port Moresby National High School under the grant program of the people of Papua New Guinea. Apart from educating the youths of PNG the school also serves as a symbol of solidarity between the two nations. The school is grateful for the continuous support recieved from the people of Japan through their government. In 2011 when the Great East Japan Earthquake and Tsunami devastated the people of Japan, Port Moresby National High School students with the help of their families contributed with cash and kind, which was donated to a school in Japan that was affected by the disaster.

The school is now 30 years old and the relationship between POMNATHS and the Japanese Government is strong and growing. In this year alone, two separate groups of POMNATHS students were give the opportunity of travelling to Japan to attend a Science Fair and the Youth Ambassadors for World Tsunami Awareness Day respectively. Port Moresby National High School still maintains a strong position as one of the top schools in the country and is adamant on living up to its motto which is "TO DO GREAT THINGS WITH GOD'S HELP".
